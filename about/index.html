About Us

ShriBooks is an online accounting system, providing a cost-effective way to manage accounting needs of growing businesses. Professionals and Small/Medium Businesses performing financial transactions need to maintain accounting data for statutory reporting and statistical analysis. ShriBooks aims to provide such features to easily record and analyze data in a collaborative way, so that customers can focus more on their core expertise to grow their business.

We are based in Bengaluru, with a core team having experience of developing ERP application which is successfully implemented in many countries like Singapore, Malaysia, etc. Initially we started ShriBooks to keep track of personal accounting data and over the years, we have added features that are necessary for companies such as Taxation, Invoices etc. And we are continuously working towards building an improved solution fulfilling the advanced needs of our customers.
