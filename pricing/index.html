Frequently Asked Questions

Trial

How does the 7-day free trial work?

ShriBooks is completely free for 7 days. Once your trial is over, you can continue using it by activating your subscription.

Can I change plans during the trial period?

Once you start a trial, you are not able to switch to a different plan until the trial period is over. But when activating the subscription, you can change the plan.

Can I create more than one company under trial period?

Only one company using the Starter plan can be created, but you can create multiple companies using the Standard plan.

What happens if I don't activate the subscription after free trial?

Once the free trial is over, you will no longer have access to the trial company. You will need to subscribe to a plan to continue using it.

Subscription

Which payment modes do you support?

ShriBooks integrates with Razorpay payment gateway, which accepts credit cards, debit cards, net banking (India), etc.

Is there any discount on annual subscription?

Yes! In addition to monthly subscriptions, ShriBooks is also available as an annual subscription. Annual subscriptions offer a 2-month discount.

Do you store my credit card information?

No, we don't store your credit card information. Learn more about our Privacy Policy here.

Can I cancel the subscription?

We are sure that you’ll stick around for a long time. But, if you’re not satisfied with our product or service, you can cancel the subscription anytime.
